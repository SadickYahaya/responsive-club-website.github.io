About E.K.A

Enyonyam ka Erade is a youth club based in Adjako, Abokobi, a town situated at the outskirts of Accra. The club was founded in 2021, when th pandemic had hit hard. The club was founded with the purpose of providing assistance, support, entertainment and help its members live a good lifestyle.
